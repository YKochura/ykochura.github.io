Yuriy Kochura

Researcher in machine learning

About

I'm a senior lecturer at Igor Sikorsky Kyiv Polytechnic Institute working on machine learning and deep learning. I'm interested in applying AI for medical prognosis, diagnosis and treatment. Also, I'm interested in techniques that allow robots to autonomously acquire new skills with minimal supervision in real-world environments. I teach a class at Igor Sikorsky Kyiv Polytechnic Institute on Reinforcement Learning.

Now, I'm working on my PhD at Igor Sikorsky Kyiv Polytechnic Institute / Department of Computer Engineering under supervision of Prof. Yuri G. Gordienko. The thesis title is “Deep machine learning method for object identification, classification and semantic segmentation”.

Before graduate school, I studied Applied Physics at Igor Sikorsky Kyiv Polytechnic Institute, Institute of Physics and Technology. In June 2017, I graduated from Igor Sikorsky Kyiv Polytechnic Institute and obtained Master degree in Applied Physics.
